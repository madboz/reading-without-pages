About

Matter is an online publication and collaborative research project centered on the themes of media and memory.

The site acts as a catalogue of ideas that explore media and memory; such as archiving, collective memory, and memory in the digital age. Through a diverse range of article formats: long-form texts, image essays, video playlists, Matter presents ideas from different fields of study.
