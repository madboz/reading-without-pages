About

Matter is an online publication and collaborative research project centered on the theme of media and memory.

The site will act as a catalogue of ideas that explore the theme of media and memory; such as archiving and collection, collective memory, and memory in the digital age. Through a diverse range of article formats: long-form texts, image essays, video playlists, and other digital objects, Matter will present ideas from a range of fields. These would include fields like philosophy, psychology, fine art, and media theory.
